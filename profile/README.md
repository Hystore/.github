# HYSTORE Open Source Repository
HYSTORE Open Source Repository is the **common space** where the project’s open-source software, tools and technical resources are brought together to **support the development and integration of advanced thermal energy storage solutions**. 

It gives researchers, developers and industry stakeholders access to **reusable digital assets** that help enable smarter, more flexible and interoperable heating and cooling systems.

More than a project output, the repository is intended as a basis for **collaboration beyond HYSTORE**. 

By making key results openly available, it aims to **encourage reuse, further development and connection with the wider community** working on energy flexibility, digitalisation and the clean energy transition.

## Repositories
- [TES-LEC-operation](https://github.com/Hystore/TES-LEC-operation): **centralized optimization** of a multi-peer energy community with multiple energy vectors such as electricity, heat, cold, gas and biomass.
- [TES-sizing](https://github.com/Hystore/TES-sizing): **centralized, single-node energy system optimization** workflow built with Pyomo.
